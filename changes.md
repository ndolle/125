Claude Mythos significantly reduces the time required to discover vulnerabilities. What traditionally took weeks or months can now be achieved in near real time.

It also removes the need for expert-level skill in chaining vulnerabilities. Complex attack paths that once required specialised knowledge can now be generated directly by Mythos.

The barrier to discovering zero-day vulnerabilities is therefore lowered. With access to Mythos, a wider range of actors could identify previously unknown weaknesses and cause significant damage.

As a result, many traditional defensive approaches, including vulnerability management, periodic scanning, and scheduled patching, require review, as they are not designed for this speed and scale.
